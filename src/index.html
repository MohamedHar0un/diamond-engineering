WELCOME TO DIAMOND

DIAMOND is a leading Egyptian company in the manufacturing of precast concrete products, mainly interlocking pavers, curbstones, and building blocks.


Diamond Engineering is an Egyptian joint stock company specializing in the production of high-value and quality concrete products. Diamond Engineering established the Interlock Block Factory Using the modernist German technologies, and latest “State Of The Art” plants. machinery controlled by highly skilled engineers and technicians. This is in order to raise the level of quality parallelly with productivity in the promising Egyptian market and keep pace with the unprecedented urban boom in our country
